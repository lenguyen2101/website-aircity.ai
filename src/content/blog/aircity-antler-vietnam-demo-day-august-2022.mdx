The video is a pitch for AirCity, a Vietnam-based residential management platform, presented by Co-founder and CEO Nhat Le. The company aims to solve the financial, mental, and legal problems landlords face by providing a low-cost, high-tech, and stress-free management solution. Their proprietary "Newton Platform" automates operations, including a 70% automated payment process that uses QR codes and robotic receipt confirmation. AirCity targets the medium-tier property market in Vietnam, which has a total addressable market of over $1 billion. Their business model consists of a 5% monthly revenue share for management services and a 60% commission on leasing vacant rooms. The company has demonstrated strong traction with 300% growth over the past six months. The experienced team has a background in SaaS proptech and has won several awards. AirCity is currently raising a seed round to expand its services and transform cities in Southeast Asia into sustainable smart cities.
