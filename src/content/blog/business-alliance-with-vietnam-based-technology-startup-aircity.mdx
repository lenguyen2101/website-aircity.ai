*Bài viết được đăng lần đầu trên [WingArc1st Inc.](https://corp.wingarc.com/en/public/202405/news2728.html) vào ngày 2024-05-02.*

> We are looking forward to the continued technology partnership with AirCity, an alumnus of the WingArc Data Empowerment Accelerator, and growing with them to deliver our data visualization technology to the Southeast Asia property market. AirCity is an innovator in utilizing technology to drive the transformation of the property management sector, and we are confident that our partnership, including our technology contribution, will enable both companies to grow together in the ASEAN market.

> The WingArc Accelerator Program has played a vital role in helping AirCity achieve its goal of providing valuable insights to landlords. Thanks to this partnership, AirCity is proud to be the first to offer a solution that will enhance the performance of businesses and resident experience in the property market using data. The company is thrilled to have collaborated with WingArc1st and is committed to continuing to innovate and make a positive impact in the industry.

VIETNAM & SINGAPORE, May 2nd, 2024 - WingArc1st Inc. (Head Office: Minato-ku, Tokyo; President, Executive Officer and CEO: Jun Tanaka; "WingArc1st"), through its subsidiary in Singapore (WingArc Singapore) has formed a business alliance with Aircity Co. Ltd. (Head Office: Vietnam; CEO & Co-Founder: Le Hoang Nhat; "AirCity"), a property management solution operated in Vietnam. As the first step in this alliance, AirCity will on May 2nd launch its AirCity Insights service, which utilizes "MotionBoard", WingArc1st's business intelligence (BI) dashboard that leads the Japanese market.  
  
Growing beyond Vietnam's burgeoning property market, AirCity's mission is to support property owners and management firms in utilizing technology to enhance the safety, sustainability, and security of the residential facilities under management. The company currently serves over 30 clients ranging from individual landlords to B2B companies with more than 1500 paid units from 60 locations in 5 cities.  
  
AirCity is one of Vietnam's fastest-growing startups and was selected into top startup accelerator programs by leading global technology brands. Most recently, the company was awarded the Smart City Award 2023 (Intelligent Operations Center - IOC). The company participated in the WingArc Data Empowerment Accelerator in 2023, which became the catalyst for this technology and business alliance.  
  
**Summary of the service "AirCity Insights":  
**AirCity Insights provides property owners and management firms with customized data dashboards that offer valuable insights to optimize their rental business. With interactive charts, operators can easily keep track of key business metrics, including revenue, cost, tenants, and maintenance tickets, which can help them make informed decisions and improve their overall property management processes.

As "The Data Empowerment Company", WingArc1st will expand our partner networks by collaborating with various companies, both in Japan and globally.  
  
■ About AirCity  
AirCity (Headquarters: Vietnam) was founded in 2021 as a property technology startup. The company has developed an all-in-one platform for property owners to manage transactions and requests. The platform will also integrate home and life service suppliers to serve tenants' needs and property maintenance.  
  
■ About WingArc1st Inc.  
WingArc1st Inc. develops solutions that leads the Japanese market in the data-empowerment fields of form solutions and Business Intelligence (BI). We have positioned the expansion of our overseas business as one of our growth strategies and have strengthened our business in the ASEAN region by opening a subsidiary in Shanghai, China in 2009, a subsidiary in Singapore in 2014, and a subsidiary in Melbourne, Australia in 2017.
